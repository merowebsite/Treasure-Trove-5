Utilisation of Time in Present Situation…………Nistha Shah

Nistha is an enthusiastic learner and a creative thinker. She has an amazing perspective on UTILISATION OF PRESENT SITUATION EFFICIENTLY. She believes that confidence in ourselves is a key to win every problem and difficulty around us. We can win any situation despite the size of problem. This present situation when we were not prepared might have created a hurdle in our mindset. She is quite aware how the media, news and every situation might create negative impact in our life but at the same time she questions us, if all these worries are worth taking? As, present situation seems not a favorable one; the whole world is going through economic downturn, unemployment and lot of stress. Sometimes the situation goes out of control and we human have to work with strength and patience to overcome them. She questions each of us, if it is worth consuming all these negativity and destroy ourselves because of this unexpected situation? She reminds us that twenty four hours a day is good amount of time for working on our DREAMS and PASSION. She believes the magic of PASSION and making time to work for the passion is BLISS. It may be Learning to play musical instruments, learning to different language, painting as well as anything creative. This will also support for a healthy body as well as mind. As an optimist finds opportunities in every situation of life, she has figured multiple opportunities even when we are going through a tough time. The way Emerging you tubers with unlimited creativity are utilizing their time in hand is exemplary. She appreciates the young generation on their participation on online seminars and interaction which helps them to understand the world and enhance their knowledge and capacity. She is cautious that staying passive creates negativity in us. Hence, she suggests each of us to engage in anything that makes us happy and helps to reach our goals in life. Time and tide waits for none. So, she suggests us to embrace our life in every way possible in any situation.
